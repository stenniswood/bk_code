Running:

1) Turn off & unplug PS4.
2) sudo ds4drv
	No path needed.
3) Press and Hold PS button and Share buttons for 10+ seconds until rapid 
	flashing.
	The ds4drv should show the device bluetooth address.
4) if no js0 appears in /dev/input/ then plug in the controller via USB.
5) Should see /dev/input/js0
